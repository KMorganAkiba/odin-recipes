Air Fryer Potato Wedges

Ingredients

  • 2 medium Russet potatoes, cut into wedges
  • 1-1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon parsley flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

  2. Place potato wedges in a large bowl. Add olive oil, paprika,
    parsley, chili, salt, and pepper. Mix well to cobmine.

  3. Place 8 wedges in the basker of the air fryer and cook for 10 minutes.

  4. Flip wedges with tongs and cook for an additional 5 minutes. Repeat
    with the remaining 8 wedges

Nutition Facts

Per Serving: 129 calories; protein 2.3g; carbohydrates 19g
fat 5.3g; sodium 230.2mg
